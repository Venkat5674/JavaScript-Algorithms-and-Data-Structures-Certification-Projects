Palindrome Checker

A palindrome is a word or phrase that reads the same backwards as forwards, ignoring punctuation, case, and spacing.

Examples of palindromes:

  • "race car"
  • "A man, a plan, a canal. Panama"
  • "never odd or even"
  • "0_0 (: /-\ :) 0-0"
